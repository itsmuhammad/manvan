Al Reem Island is one of Abu Dhabi's most popular residential areas, with modern towers like Gate Towers, Sun Tower, and Marina Heights. Moving here requires planning—from booking elevators to understanding building rules. Here's what you need to know.

1. Book the Service Elevator in Advance

Most Al Reem Island buildings require you to book the service elevator or loading bay for your move. Contact building management at least 48–72 hours ahead. Peak times (weekends, month-end) fill up fast.

2. Choose Movers Experienced in High-Rise Moves

Moving into a tower is different from a villa. Look for movers who regularly service Al Reem Island. They'll know building layouts, parking restrictions, and how to protect common areas.

3. Factor in Distance and Time

Parking can be limited. Movers may need to use trolleys for longer distances from the van to the elevator. Allow extra time for high-rise moves, especially during busy periods.
