Abu Dhabi to Dubai moving cost depends on route, volume and access at both ends. A single sofa pickup is very different from a 2-BHK apartment with packing, dismantling and tower lift bookings.

What changes the intercity quote?

Distance, crew size, vehicle size, tolls, fuel, waiting time, packing, dismantling and parking distance all affect price. The more accurate your inventory, the more accurate your quote.

When a van is enough

A van can work well for single items, marketplace furniture, studio loads and compact apartment moves. Full homes, large wardrobes or multiple bedrooms may need a truck or more movers.

How to avoid delays

Confirm building access in Abu Dhabi and Dubai, reserve lifts, share contact details for both locations and keep fragile items labelled separately.
