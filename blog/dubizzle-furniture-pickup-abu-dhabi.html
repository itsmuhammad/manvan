Buying second-hand furniture can save money, but pickup is where many deals become stressful. A Dubizzle furniture pickup Abu Dhabi job needs the same planning as a small move: correct vehicle, enough manpower, lift access, wrapping and clear timing with both seller and buyer.

Confirm the item before you book

Ask the seller for photos, measurements and whether the item can be dismantled. A sofa, wardrobe, dining table, bed frame or fridge may look simple online but still require two movers, tools or careful wrapping. Measure the lift, corridor and doorway if the item is bulky.

Agree pickup timing with the seller

Many Abu Dhabi towers require service-lift booking or security approval. Confirm the seller has permission to move the item out, especially in Al Reem Island, Corniche, Al Raha Beach and Yas Island apartment buildings. If the seller is in a villa, check gate access and parking.

Protect the item before loading

Used furniture often has exposed corners, loose hardware or glass panels. Ask for blanket wrapping, stretch wrap or cardboard corner protection where neeservice. For appliances, keep the item upright, secure loose parts and avoid dragging across common-area flooring.

What to send the mover

  • Seller and buyer locations.
  • Photos and measurements of the item.
  • Floor level and lift details at both sides.
  • Whether dismantling or assembly is required.
  • Any building timing restrictions.
