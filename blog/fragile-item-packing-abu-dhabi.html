Fragile item packing in Abu Dhabi is one of the highest-value parts of a move. Most damage happens when glass, electronics or decor are packed too quickly, loaded under heavier boxes, or left without clear labels.

Items that need extra protection

Glassware, plates, mirrors, TVs, artwork, lamps, marble tops, ceramics and delicate decor need individual wrapping and a stable carton. Large fragile items may need blankets, edge guards or custom handling.

What professional packers do differently

  • Use cartons sized for the item, not oversized boxes.
  • Wrap each fragile item separately.
  • Fill empty space so items do not shift.
  • Label room, contents and top side clearly.

When fragile-only packing is enough

If you can pack clothes, books and personal items yourself, fragile-only packing can keep cost controlled while still protecting the items that matter most.
