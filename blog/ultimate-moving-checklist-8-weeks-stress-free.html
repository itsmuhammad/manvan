Ultimate Moving Checklist: 8 Weeks to Stress-Free

A week-by-week guide to help you prepare for your move without the last-minute rush.

Moving doesn't have to be chaotic. Follow this 8-week checklist for a smooth transition to your new home.

Weeks 8-6: Planning Phase

Research moving companies, get quotes, and start decluttering. Notify your current landlord if renting.

Weeks 5-4: Preparation

Order packing supplies, start packing non-essentials, and arrange utilities at your new address.

Weeks 3-2: Major Packing

Pack room by room. Label all boxes clearly. Confirm moving date with your chosen movers.

Week 1: Final Week

Pack essentials last. Defrost fridge, clean your old home, and prepare your "first night" box.

Moving Day

Do a final walkthrough, hand over keys, and head to your new home. Unpack essentials first.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I start packing for a move?

Start 8 weeks out. Begin with non-essentials in weeks 8–6, move to major packing in weeks 5–2, and pack essentials only in the final week. This prevents last-minute chaos.

What is a first-night box and what should it contain?

A first-night box holds everything you need immediately after moving — toiletries, a change of clothes, phone charger, important documents, kettle, mugs, and a basic snack. Keep it in your car, not the moving van.

When should I book movers in Abu Dhabi?

Book movers in weeks 8–6 of your planning phase, or at least 2–3 weeks before your move date. Month-end and summer weekends fill up fast — book early to secure your preferred slot.
