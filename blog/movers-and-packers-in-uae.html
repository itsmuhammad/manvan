What a serious moving quote should include

Ask for confirmation of the vehicle, number of movers, packing materials, dismantling, reassembly, fuel, waiting time and access assumptions. This is especially important for tower moves and intercity routes.

When to use full movers and packers

Full packing is useful for villas, larger apartments, offices and fragile homes. For single items, studio moves and Dubizzle pickups, a smaller man with van Abu Dhabi service can be faster and more affordable.

Areas and routes to clarify

Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Khalifa City, Al Reem Island, Mussafah, Yas Island and Mohammed Bin Zayed City all have different access and timing issues. Share the exact building or community name before accepting the quote.
