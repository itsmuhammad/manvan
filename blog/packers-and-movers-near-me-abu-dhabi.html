When packing support makes sense

Use professional packing for glassware, mirrors, TVs, artwork, kitchen items, wardrobes, office files and fragile decor. A good packing team brings cartons, wrap, tape, labels and furniture protection.

Full packing or fragile-only packing?

Full packing is best for villas, large apartments and office moves. Fragile-only packing is enough when you can pack clothes and personal items yourself but need help with breakables, electronics or furniture.

What to confirm before booking

  • How many cartons and materials are included.
  • Whether furniture dismantling is included.
  • Whether packing happens before or on moving day.
  • How fragile items will be labelled and loaded.
