Our Story

Founservice in 2014, Man With Van started with a simple mission: to make moving stress-free for everyone in Abu Dhabi. What began as a small team with one van has grown into the region's most trusted moving company.

Over the years, we've moved thousands of families into their dream homes and helped countless businesses relocate without disrupting their operations. Our success comes from our unwavering commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction.

Today, we're proud to be Abu Dhabi's premier moving service, with a fleet of modern vehicles, a team of trained professionals, and a track record that speaks for itself.
